Basement Floor Epoxy

Basement floor epoxy in Columbus addresses two problems at once: bare concrete that looks unfinished and moisture that seeps up through the slab. Our moisture-mitigating primer blocks vapor transmission before we apply the coating system, preventing the blistering and delamination that kills cheaper coatings in below-grade spaces. A coated basement floor is easy to sweep, resists water from minor flooding, and makes the whole space feel cleaner and brighter.


Popular finishes for basements include solid color with a satin topcoat (clean, understated) and decorative chip (hides scuffs, high durability). Call us today for basement floor epoxy in Columbus.

Metallic Epoxy Flooring

Metallic epoxy creates a swirled, three-dimensional appearance using metallic pigment particles suspended in the epoxy base coat. No two floors look the same. It's the most visually striking option we offer — common applications include home gyms, showrooms, upscale garages, retail boutiques, and restaurant lobbies. Metallic systems are installed in the same multi-step process as standard epoxy, with the metallic layer applied between the primer and the clear topcoat. Pricing runs $5–$9 per square foot installed.


If you're looking for something beyond a standard solid color or chip floor, call us and ask about our metallic epoxy options. We bring color samples to every estimate.

Decorative Flake & Quartz Systems

Decorative flake (also called chip or broadcast systems) is one of the most popular finishes for garages and commercial kitchens. Colored vinyl flakes are broadcast into the wet base coat, then sealed under a clear topcoat. The result is a speckled surface that hides minor scuffs, adds depth, and provides natural anti-slip texture. Quartz systems embed colored quartz aggregate for a harder, more uniform surface — commonly used in bathrooms, pool decks, and commercial kitchens where hygiene and slip resistance are priorities.


Both systems are available in dozens of color blends. We stock the most popular combinations and can order custom blends with a short lead time.
